## **Cralee's Magnetic Launcher**

*5th-level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (magnetised iron worth at least 1 sp)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Make a ranged spell attack against a Medium or smaller creature within range. If the target is a construct or wearing metal armour, you have advantage on the attack roll. A beam of crackling energy streaks toward the target.
