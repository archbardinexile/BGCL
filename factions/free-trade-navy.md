# Affiliations

## **Combe Shipping and Courier Agency**

The Combe Shipping and Courier Agency is one the largest business empires in the Relief. Operating out of the [Duchy of Ruby](../locations/profundus#duchy-of-ruby), the agency's grand fleet of ships and airships deliver goods with speed and security. In [992 PO](../timeline#992-po) the CSCA were thwarted by a civilian blockade of [Anchora](../locations/contritus#anchora), preventing their expansion into Eastern [Gwerdowra](../locations/contritus#gwerdowra). This led to the founding of the Free Trade Navy.

## **The Saltmoot**

The Saltmoot is a loose alliance of pirates, privateers and merchant crews governed by those with the largest fleets and the greatest wealth. The city state of [Libertalia](../locations/contritus#libertalia) serves as headquarters for the ever-changing members of a pirate conclave that keep the various guilds of the [Manyshells](../locations/contritus#the-manyshells) in check.
