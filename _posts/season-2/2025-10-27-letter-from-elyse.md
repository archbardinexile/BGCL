A letter from Elyse Trelawney to Guy Brush.

> FTN Marley, Glasdowra. 996, Inceptum 13th.
>
> Guy Brush.
>
> I sincerely hope you are alive to receive this letter so that you may come to understand how much I have on my mind without you weighing on it further. Why must you be so enigmatic? Having said that, I find myself in the lamentable position of needing your help. Though for what I cannot say by letter.

> Elyse.
